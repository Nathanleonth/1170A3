About Me

Hi, my name is Nathan, I am from Dubai and I have come to Canada to pursue a bachelor's Degree in Computer Science.
I have always had an interest in computers. As a kid, I would love to experiment with different software. I learned programming by first understanding the basics of loops, algorithms and the basic syntax in Pseudocode. Then I moved on to Python as it is an easy language to learn for beginners. I finished my CS course in school where I had to give Exams and Practicums based on Pseudocode and Python. After that, I came to Dalhousie because it was always my parent's dream to send me to Canada for university. I took Computer Science because I had a great understanding and a lot of interest in the field. I took CSCI 1110 and switched from Python to Java in a week. Most of my projects are from 1110 Assignments.

I found CSCI 1109 interesting as it was a new way to play with data. I learnt that you can use Python modules to extract data from huge datasets containing thousands of rows and columns. It made me realize how strong our computers will be in the coming years.
